Welcome to the Junior Lawyers Division website

The JLD represents approximately 75,000 individuals. Membership is automatic for LPC students and graduates, trainee solicitors and solicitors up to five years qualified. Through our Executive Committee, National Committee, and network of local groups, we engage with our members on both a political and social level.
